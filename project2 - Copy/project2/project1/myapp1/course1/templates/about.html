Who We Are

From Humble Beginnings

Founded in 2010, FoodExpress started as a small family-owned restaurant with just 5 tables. Our founder, Chef Marco, had a simple vision: to bring authentic, delicious food to our community using the freshest ingredients.

Today, we've grown to become one of the most beloved food delivery services in the region, but we've never lost sight of our roots. Every dish we prepare carries the same love and attention to detail that we started with.
